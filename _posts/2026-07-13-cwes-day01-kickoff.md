## Why log this publicly

- Anything I pwn along the way gets a normal writeup under `Writeups`,
  cross-linked from that day's entry.
- Everything reusable — commands, payloads, checklists — gets folded into the
  [running cheatsheet](/cwes-cheatsheet/), which should be exam-ready by day 60.
