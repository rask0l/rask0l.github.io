Welcome to the site. This is where I document my journey through offensive
security — Hack The Box, CTFs, and whatever rabbit holes I fall into along the way.

<!--more-->

## Why a public blog?

Two reasons:

1. **Writing forces understanding.** You can follow a walkthrough and root a box
   without really learning anything. Explaining it — every command, every dead
   end — is where it actually sticks.
2. **Notes for future-me (and maybe you).** At 2am, stuck on a box, a good writeup
   is gold. I want to give back to the same well I keep drinking from.

## What lives here

- **[Writeups]({{ '/writeups/' | relative_url }})** — clean, reproducible box
  walkthroughs. Enumeration to root, no skipped steps.
- **[Blog]({{ '/blog/' | relative_url }})** — this. Ideas, tooling, and thinking
  out loud.
- **[Progress]({{ '/progress/' | relative_url }})** — a living `/now` page of what
  I'm grinding on.

## A note on ethics

Everything here targets machines I'm **authorized** to attack — HTB, CTFs, and my
own lab. Security knowledge is for defenders and the curious, not for pointing at
things you don't own.
