Setting this up so I have somewhere to put my notes.

Mostly this will be Hack The Box and CTF writeups — full walkthroughs, from the
first `nmap` scan to root, including the things I got wrong on the way. The blog
side is for everything else: tools I'm trying, stuff I'm reading, ideas I want to
come back to.

Writing it down helps me actually learn it. And if one of these ever helps
someone who's stuck, even better.

Everything here targets machines I'm allowed to attack. That's the only rule.
